Nox VPN - Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026

1. Introduction

Nox VPN ("we", "our", "the app") is a free VPN application that uses the WireGuard protocol to provide secure internet connectivity. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

2. Data We Collect

Nox VPN collects minimal data necessary for VPN functionality:

  • WireGuard Key Pair: A public/private key pair is generated locally on your device. The private key never leaves your device. The public key is sent to Cloudflare WARP API during initial registration only.
  • Device Type: Basic device information (e.g., "Android") is sent during WARP registration to obtain VPN configuration.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not log your browsing activity or traffic.
  • We do not collect your IP address, DNS queries, or connection timestamps.
  • We do not collect personal information such as name, email, or phone number.
  • We do not use analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.
  • We do not share any data with third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

The app uses Cloudflare WARP infrastructure for VPN connectivity. When you use the VPN, your internet traffic is routed through Cloudflare's network. Cloudflare's own privacy policy governs how they handle network traffic. You can review it at cloudflare.com/privacypolicy.

5. Data Storage

All configuration data (VPN keys, server settings, user preferences) is stored locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences. No data is stored on external servers by us.

6. Data Security

VPN connections are encrypted using the WireGuard protocol, which employs state-of-the-art cryptography including Curve25519 for key exchange, ChaCha20 for encryption, and Poly1305 for authentication.

7. Children's Privacy

Nox VPN is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us by opening an issue on our GitHub repository or contacting us at the email provided on our Google Play listing.
